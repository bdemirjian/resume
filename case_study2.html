Agile Transformation | 25 Teams | 3 Offices | One Year

CURRENT MANAGEMENT PROCESSES | Inconsistent at best, Business Unit interfaces were challenging and avoided. Pain-derived big-bang releases happened quarterly and were overnight affairs.

SLOTHLIKE | This was the most colorful word used by our largest customer to describe our pace of general software development. Some of our products suffered from a myriad of legacy-based issues.

FINDINGS

DISTRIBUTION AND TIMING

Week-long training, including specialized PO portion, was conducted in two waves in each location.

END-STATE

3x+ KUMBAYA? | Absolutely! Within six months of training, HQ-based teams had 2X their throughput with much heavier testing requirements. Within a year the HQ-based teams were at 3.5x their previous throughput.
Customer engagement went from two regular attendees of our bi-weekly demonstrations to over 250, spanning more than 50 companies. This led to many of our customers investing in us, accelerating our growth, and reducing our time to market.

STABILIZED THE TERMINAL PATIENT | One of our products was in really bad shape. While being a market leader, the technology behind it was embarrassingly bad. These business units rebounded within six months and they were reporting better customer outcomes. Further, they were on a path to modern architecture within 18 months.
