CONTEXT

NEW CIO AND DRIVE TO MODERNIZE | New CIO arrives with plans to modernized management methods as well as technology.

Business & Technology Transformation | 15 Teams | 4 Offices | One Year

CURRENT BUSINESS PROCESSES | Strong Sales and Marketing business units with a weak product unit yielded no long-term vision or goals. Additionally, work managment was not based on a prioritation schema.

TECHNOLOGY | Our backend is based on Perl, an almost extinct language, we find it hard to locate effective developers. We are in process of strangling it off into a modern Java based architecture.

FINDINGS

OPPORTUNTIES | Business Intelligence partners revealed a number of opportunities lurking in our traffic data.
R&D Teams reveal acclerators and easy integrations, that unlock cross-pillar-sales.

PRODUCT & MARKETING MISALIGNMENT | Product and marketing are at odds, The new vision is hard to manifest, Strong facilitation is required.

END-STATE

TRAFFIC! ADDONS PAGE | Fitting the context of our use-case the churn and where it was in our flow the tests with a dedicated Addon page kept winning.
This accelerated the growth of our high-profit products as we were able to optimize against this low-cost high churn resource.

EXPANDED OPPORTUNTY SPACE | The cross-pillar sales experiment opened a whole vista to be optimized. Our Google Analytics SMEs helped us start to categorize these opportunities and potential tradeoffs.

EMERGENT WIN CONDITIONS

EMPOWERED TEAMS | CIO chose to invest in more force-multipliers to assist in forming empowered teams. Teams now included Business, Technology, Data Intelligence partners. Standups include traffic dashboards and test strategy discussions. The feed-back loops are so fast that in prod changes are occuring the same day in many cases.
